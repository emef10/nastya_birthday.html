✦ A message

Dear Nastya,

Written from far away, felt from the heart

Somewhere across the distance, on the day you turn 19, I wanted to create something that says what words in a message sometimes can't quite carry.

You're someone who lives fully — the girl who holds full concerts in her room with just a playlist and her own spirit, who gets completely absorbed in a Turkish drama as though it's her own life unfolding. Someone who chose medicine because she wants to save lives. That's not a small thing, Nastya. That's everything.

You've grown up in a time and place that has asked far too much of you. Yet here you are — still curious, still warm, still laughing. The strength in that is something I deeply admire.

Distance is real, and I feel it. But so is this: that I think about you, that your smile lives somewhere in my mind, and that today, on your birthday, I wanted you to know that somewhere in Istanbul, someone is very glad you exist.

— Emre 🌸
